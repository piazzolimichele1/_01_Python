
# Live Strategies

109 live trading strategies across three US indices: NAS100, US30 and SP500.
Every strategy runs on its own Python engine (MT5 connection, signal generation,
order execution, trade management) and carries its own results on two brokers,
demo on Alpari and prop on Alpha Capital Group.

## Strategy distribution
- NAS100: 71
- US30: 14
- SP500: 24

## Inside each strategy
- Python engine (MT5 connection, signal, execution, trade management)
- Demo and prop reports (.html + trades CSV)
- Demo and prop risk configuration
